About Insanity gym

Insanity gym is a multi-level facility that houses an expansive selection of equipment, and resources for a complete wellness experience. We provide a friendly, welcoming atmosphere for all members for all fitness levels from beginners to the PRO athletes. Whether your passion is, cardio, weight training, bodybuilding, powerlifting, or functional training; we have all the equipment and necessary machines you need for results.

  • Over 18,000 square feet of space
  • Locker rooms with private showers and day lockers
  • Two levels of cardio equipment
  • 60 feet of turf for sleds
  • Dumbbells up to 150 lbs
  • Hammer Strength plate loaded equipment
  • Pin loaded weight training machines
  • 7 squat racks
  • 4 deadlift platforms with bumper plates
  • Rogue Glute Hamstring Developer
